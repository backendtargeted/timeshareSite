Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about timeshare exits.

How long does the timeshare exit process take? +

The average timeshare exit process takes 6-12 months, depending on the complexity of your situation and the cooperation of the resort. We'll provide you with a more specific timeline during your consultation.

What are the costs involved? +

Our fees vary based on your specific timeshare situation. We offer a free consultation to evaluate your case and provide a quote. Remember, we don't charge any upfront fees, and we offer a money-back guarantee if we cannot cancel your timeshare.

Can I exit my timeshare on my own? +

While it's technically possible to exit a timeshare on your own, the process can be complex and time-consuming. Most resorts make it deliberately difficult to cancel contracts. Our expertise increases your chances of success and saves you the hassle of navigating this complicated process.

Will this affect my credit score? +

Our legal exit strategies are designed to minimize any impact on your credit score. During your consultation, we'll discuss your specific situation and outline the best approach for your circumstances.

What makes you different from other exit companies? +
